## COPY PRINCIPLES APPLIED

- **No "transformative journey" language.** This is practical, confident, human.
- **Lead with time and stress saved**, not just dollars. Most people's biggest constraint is decision fatigue, not budget.
- **"We did the homework"** positions SALTY as the smart friend who figured it all out, not a corporation selling a package.
- **Source link transparency** is the trust builder. "Check for yourself" is more persuasive than "trust us."
- **Community framing** is woven throughout, not bolted on. The "30 instant friends" card does more selling than any price comparison.
- **The framing shifts based on savings level.** Strong savings = lead with dollars. Weak savings = lead with experience and convenience.
- **"Show up and we handle everything"** is the core promise repeated in different ways.
